STORY

BASED ON TRUE EVENTS

We Were Soldiers is a Vietnam war movie that is based off the novel We Were Soldiers Once... And Young. The book written by Lt. Colonel Harold G. Moore and Joseph L. Galloway, Two men that fought in the Battle of la Drang.

The Battle of la Drang was one of the major battles in Vietnam and took place November 14th-17th 1965 in the la Drang Valley. The movie depicts a stranded division of Vietnam soldiers who are accidentally misled into a Vietcong basecamp. Although the men are surrounded and helplessly outnumbered 400 to 4000, they manage to hold their ground with the help of air support and artillery and eventually end up defeating the Vietcong.

The movie very closely matches how We Were Soldiers Once... And Young described the battle. In fact Harold G. Moore, the Author of We Were Soldies Once... And Young, described the movie as the only one to "get it right." This made the film very realistic and historically accurate.
